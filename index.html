Who I Am

My name is Joshua Luna, but in the gaming world, I go by Maiden Honor — a proud and passionate content creator for Honor of Kings.

I had been waiting for this game for years — ever since I first saw King of Glory, the original Chinese version of Honor of Kings, which was released in China nearly 10 years ago. I remember watching gameplay videos online, completely amazed, thinking, “I wish I could play this one day.” The graphics, the mechanics, the heroes — it was exactly the kind of game I had always wanted to play. But back then, it was only available in China, and all I could do was watch from the side.

So when I found out that the international version — Honor of Kings — was finally being tested in Brazil, I didn’t even think twice. I wasn’t from Brazil, but that didn’t stop me. I found a way to download it just so I could finally experience the game I had been waiting for nearly a decade.

And the moment I launched the app… it was everything I hoped for. The music, the heroes, the smooth combat — it all felt so alive. I fell in love instantly.

But then reality hit me. My phone couldn’t handle the game perfectly. It lagged a few times during matches, which made it hard to enjoy fully. Eventually, I had to stop playing. It honestly broke my heart to let it go after waiting for so long.

Months later, I finally got a new phone — and you already know what the first app I downloaded was: Honor of Kings. I still remember whispering to myself, “I’m back.” This wasn’t just a game anymore — it was something I had a deep connection to. Something that mattered to me.

Then came the moment that changed everything — June 20, 2024, when Honor of Kings officially launched globally, including here in the Philippines. The moment I saw it live, I made a decision: “I’m not just going to play this game — I’m going to share it. I’m going to help grow the community.”

That same day, I created a new TikTok account and started posting Honor of Kings content — gameplay clips, highlights, funny moments, edits, anything I could. I didn’t expect much. I just wanted to express my love for the game and maybe convince a few people to try it out. But to my surprise, in just two months, I had already gained over 1,000 followers. For someone starting completely from scratch, that was a huge milestone.

And then something incredible happened.
On December 3, 2024, the official Honor of Kings Malaysia TikTok account messaged me. They saw one of my videos, liked it, and asked if they could boost it. I was stunned. My hands were literally shaking. It was the first time any official game account had noticed my content — and that moment changed everything for me.

Just three days later, on December 6, I got another message — this time from Honor of Kings Philippines. They also wanted to boost one of my videos. I couldn’t believe it. Two official regional teams saw my work and appreciated it enough to support it. I was beyond grateful.

And then came the biggest surprise.
On December 26, 2024, just after Christmas, Honor of Kings Philippines messaged me again — but this time, it wasn’t about a single video. They asked if I wanted to become part of their official content creator program.

I was speechless. I only had around 2,000 followers at the time. I wasn’t a big creator. But they still saw something in me. They believed in me. And that meant more than I could ever put into words. It reminded me that even small creators, as long as we’re genuine and passionate, can be noticed.

Today, I’m still creating content for Honor of Kings — not because I have to, but because I genuinely love it. I want to help new players, celebrate the community, highlight epic moments, and make people smile through my content.

Thank you for visiting my page and for taking the time to get to know my story. Whether you’re a fellow player, a new fan, or someone just curious — I appreciate you. This is only the beginning, and I’m excited for what’s ahead.
See you on the battlefield. ⚔️👑
— Maiden Honor

About & Contact

Hi, I’m Joshua Luna
I’m a first-year Computer Science student at Cavite State University CCAT Campus. I specialize in problem-solving and am building strong foundations in programming and web development technologies.
My journey began at Rosario Institute, where I took the ICT strand specialized in Computer Programming during Senior High School. There, I studied HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL, which became the foundation of my skills in both frontend and backend development.

We didn’t just learn in theory — we built projects: a website, a small database system, and a library management system with borrowing, returning, and book record management. These hands-on experiences gave me insight into how digital systems work and how to bring ideas to life through code.

Recognizing my interest and potential, my programming teacher encouraged me to pursue Computer Science in college. That recommendation helped me focus on what I truly enjoy — coding, problem-solving, and creating systems that are both functional and user-friendly.

Now in college at Cavite State University - CCAT, I continue to apply what I’ve learned through more complex projects. One of our major outputs so far is a restaurant management system with features like:
  • Menu and category management
  • Order tracking with pending, finished, and canceled states
  • A system analysis panel that displays key statistics for operational insights
Each project has deepened my understanding of system design and problem-solving through code.

Looking ahead, my dream is to work at Tencent, a company that inspires me deeply — not just because of its global success in gaming, but because of its relentless innovation across industries. Tencent’s story motivates me to aim high and contribute to impactful, transformative projects.

This portfolio reflects who I am, what I’ve learned, and where I’m headed. Thanks for visiting and learning about my journey!
